About the Lab

The Design-is-ALL Lab investigates the fundamental mechanisms underlying learning-enabled self-organization. Our research focuses on designing trustworthy, intelligent, and autonomous systems that follow engineering principles and physical laws.

Our work supports robot swarms, smart manufacturing, human-robot collaboration, and autonomous vehicles.
